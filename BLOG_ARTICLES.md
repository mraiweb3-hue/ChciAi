## SEO Optimization Requirements:

### Every article must have:
- ✅ Target keyword in H1
- ✅ Target keyword in first 100 words
- ✅ 2-3 H2 headings with keyword variations
- ✅ Internal links (3+ to other pages/articles)
- ✅ External links (2-3 authority sources)
- ✅ Meta description (150-160 chars)
- ✅ Alt texts on images
- ✅ FAQ schema if applicable
- ✅ Readable URLs (e.g., /blog/ai-asistent-navratnost)
- ✅ CTA (call to action) at end

### Writing style:
- Czech language (formal but friendly)
- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
- Bullet points for lists
- Bold key points
- Use numbers/statistics
- Real examples
- Conversational tone (not corporate)
